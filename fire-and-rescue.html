Agency
Fire and Rescue NSW State Fire & Rescue

Fire and Rescue NSW (FRNSW) is the primary fire and emergency service for urban areas in New South Wales, responsible for protecting life, property and the environment in cities, larger towns and key industrial areas.

At a Glance
  • Established in 1884 (formerly NSW Fire Brigades).
  • Operates approximately 335 fire stations across NSW.
  • Responds to more than 130,000 incidents each year.
